Withholding Tax on Payments

Withholding Taxes can be created and seen from the same menu as of the normal taxes.

After Module installation, a check box “is withholding” becomes visible on a normal tax form in taxes configuration. If the tax being created is a withholding tax then this “is withholding” box should be checked.

On payment form, a special field namely “Tax Type” becomes visible in which all withholding taxes’ suggestions can be seen out of which desired tax can be selected.

Note: On a payment with no withholding tax, a withholding of 0% rate should be created and selected.

After the payment is validated, a journal entry is passed on backend in which Bank/cash is credited net of withholding tax amount.
